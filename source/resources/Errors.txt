en~vlcInitError~
en~vlcInitError~Possible reasons can be:
en~vlcInitError~  All Operating Systems:
en~vlcInitError~    1. VLC Player ist not installed
en~vlcInitError~    2. Invalid program option in args.csv
en~vlcInitError~       Where to find args.csv:
en~vlcInitError~         C:\users\<user>\CyberTelly\
en~vlcInitError~         /home/<user>/CyberTelly/
en~vlcInitError~  MacOS:    
en~vlcInitError~    VLC.App has wrong app architecture.
en~vlcInitError~    Correct architecture is Apple Silicon.
en~vlcInitError~  Linux:
en~vlcInitError~    VLC was installed from Snap Store or
en~vlcInitError~    Flathub and runs in a sandbox, where
en~vlcInitError~    the access to the VLC library is blocked.
en~vlcInitError~    Solution:
en~vlcInitError~      See file InstallationGuide.txt, which
en~vlcInitError~      can be found in the download area on
en~vlcInitError~      https://www.cybertelly.tv
